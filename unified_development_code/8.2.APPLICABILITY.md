#### 8.2.   APPLICABILITY

   This article applies to all proposed land divisions, land spits, and subdivisions of land as defined in this code, with the following exceptions:

8.2.1.   The sale or exchange of parcels of land to or between adjoining property owners if such sale or exchange does not create additional lots ;

8.2.2.   The partitioning of land in accordance with other statutes regulating the partitioning of land held in common ownership;

8.2.3.   The leasing of apartments, offices, stores, or similar space within a building or mobile home/RV park, or mineral, oil, or gas leases;

8.2.4.   Land divisions for defining cemetery plots or columbarium units; and,

8.2.5.   Although exempt from this article, these types of land divisions may still require review and approval by the PDSD staff and outside review agencies in accordance with other articles of the UDC .
